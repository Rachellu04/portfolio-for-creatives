Overview

Problem

Holistic healing has always been a large part of Eastern Culture. However, in the West, this type of healing is often overlooked or diminished as a lesser form of medicine.

Solution

To brand a high end Chinese soup restaurant that utilizes holistic healing methods in a fresh and modern way. The goal is to legitimize the art of food-based wellness through a fine dining experience.
