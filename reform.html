Reform was a project created to support and empower women in the trades. The goal is to provide outreach services that help to guide young girls throughout their journey through the male dominated trades workforce.

Through vigorous research, and developing a strong, conceptual visual identity system inspired by heat treated metal, reform aims to reshape how the trades views women.
