During a 4 week advertising workshop, I was tasked with creating a 360 campaign for a randomized product and target audience.

I developed an ad campaign for a running shoe brand targeting young adults, using a range of creative ideation techniques. The campaign centers on the exhilarating sensation of speed, inviting customers to experience that same feeling through the shoe.
