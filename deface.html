DEFACE makeup is created for the men who won’t play by the rules. In a culture obsessed with conformity, DEFACE becomes the open invitation to let go and redefine the look on your own terms.

The goal of DEFACE is to create a makeup brand that empowers men to express themselves openly and confidently. Instead of feeling the need to hide or stay discreet, this brand gives them the freedom to show up authentically and embrace makeup as a natural form of self-expression.
