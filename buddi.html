It can be stressful when children are sick. Doctor's appointments, at home care can be all too much on the busy 9-5 schedule. Buddi is an app that is designed to act as a helpful guide to help parent's through these stressful times.

This app contains information on symptoms, at home care, and systems to track doctor's appointments and the sickness progress. Manage everything, all at once, from the comfort of your own home.

View Full Case Study

The app’s visual language is light, airy, and approachable, giving parents a reassuring companion they can turn to when navigating daunting questions about their child’s health.
