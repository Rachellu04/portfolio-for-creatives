Chirp Protein is a cricket-based protein supplement developed to support diverse athletic needs. The branding explores energy, movement, and sustainability, translating the product’s high-performance purpose into a bold visual identity.

This project includes packaging design, visual language, and promotional materials built around the idea of fueling athletes with a cleaner, more efficient source of protein.

View Full Case Study

The app’s visual language is light, airy, and approachable, giving parents a reassuring companion they can turn to when navigating daunting questions about their child’s health.
