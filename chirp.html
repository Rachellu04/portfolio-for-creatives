Problem

Currently, many animal protein powder blends contribute greatly to pollution. Sustainable alternatives such as plant-based powders are unable to deliver readily available amino acids for maximum performance.

Solution

To create a compelling brand that markets cricket-based protein powder blends to athletes. The blends are crafted with performance and environmental sustainability in mind to give athletes clean, quality fuel. The brand bridges the gap between sustainable living and athletic performance, redefining how consumers view alternative protein sources.

The project began with a simple moodboard inspired by the art of sport. The branding captures the excitement and vibrancy of being on court, while the refined serif headings add an elevated twist that sets Chirp apart from competitors

Inspired by track lines and rising sound, the Chirp's logo pays tribute to sport and the energy release delivered by protein powders.


Chirp Protein’s visual identity takes its inspiration from the pulses and rhythms of sport. Soundwave graphics on the packaging represent the distinct blends of protein formulas for all types of athletic needs.
