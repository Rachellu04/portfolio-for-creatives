Tang Fang is a Chinese herbal soup restaurant that uses ancient Traditional Chinese Medicine techniques in their recipes in order to create delicious soups that not only heal the body, but also the soul.

This project includes a visual identity, packaging design, and menu design. The goal of the project was to bridge the gap between non-traditional healing methods and Eastern culture with Western dining.

Tang Fang’s visual identity draws from traditional Chinese art and design, reinterpreted through a modern lens. Warm tones paired with black create a sense of refined elevation, balanced with an underlying feeling of care.

The take-home soups allow customers to carry that healing comfort home. As Chinese parents often remind us, nothing heals quite like food.

The menu design follows the minimalist style of the brand. The binding for the book is inspired by traditional Chinese book binding.
