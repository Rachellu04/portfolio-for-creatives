Problem

How might we help culturally diverse university students build meaningful connections and feel a sense of belonging in their shared living spaces?

Solution

To provide a digital platform that goes beyond surface-level roommate matching to help students connect through shared living and cultural exchange.

After conducting market research and developing the brand strategy, we created the visual identity. Pangea’s look is designed to capture the authenticity of everyday living, building trust and fostering real, candid connections with our audience.

*app interface design by Rayna Lai

In addition to connecting students together through the app, pangea partners with universities to host in-person events on campus. This allows the connection to move offline into real-life in order to foster lasting connections.

I designed a website where our brand guide lives. This ensures that all designers have access to the brand guidelines in the increasingly digital world.
